Hao Su Lab
Undergraduate Researcher · Jun 2025 – Present
UC San Diego · Cross-embodied co-design for dexterous hands
  • Parametric hand generator and simulation pipeline for 10,000+ hand designs.
  • Morphology-conditioned PPO with RNN backbone, action masking, and adaptive domain randomization.
  • Graph heuristic search + GNN value function for ranking partial and complete designs, achieving ~400× faster evaluation than per-morphology training.
  • Sim-to-real deployment: motor tuning, gains, and Python control mapping.
Rose Lab (ERSP)
Undergraduate Researcher · Sep 2024 – Jun 2025
Surrogate modeling & Bayesian Active Learning
  • Extended TGLF-NN in PyTorch to approximate nonlinear plasma turbulence models with test MSE ≈ 0.03 on ~670k simulations.
  • Designed a BAL framework with Expected Information Gain matching full-data accuracy using ~35% of the data.
  • Deployed experiments on Nautilus with Docker, Kubernetes, W&B logging, and S3 storage.

Triton Humanoid
Jul 2025 – Present
UCSD’s first scratch-built humanoid · Disturbance-rejection & locomotion with PPO
  • Modeled a custom humanoid from mechanical CAD into a physics simulator with realistic inertias, joint limits, and contacts.
  • Designed RL environments and trained locomotion policies with adaptive domain randomization and privileged observations.
  • Distilled a privileged teacher into a student policy using only onboard sensing for deployment.
  • Integrated JIT-compiled PyTorch policies into a ROS2 control stack with sensor and motor nodes.

Robotic Arm Manipulation (ARCTOS)
Mar 2025 – May 2025
6-DoF arm · Imitation learning on top of IK
  • 3D-printed and assembled a 6-DoF ARCTOS arm and modeled it in MuJoCo (MJCF).
  • Implemented an IK controller and scripted expert to generate cube-grasp trajectories as demos.
