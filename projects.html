Triton Humanoid
Jul 2025 – Present
UCSD's first scratch-built humanoid · Disturbance-rejection & locomotion with PPO
  • Modeled a custom humanoid from mechanical CAD into a physics simulator with realistic inertias, joint limits, and contacts.
  • Designed RL environments and trained locomotion policies with adaptive domain randomization and privileged observations.
  • Distilled a privileged teacher into a student policy using only onboard sensing for deployment.
  • Integrated JIT-compiled PyTorch policies into a ROS2 control stack with sensor and motor nodes.

Robotic Arm Manipulation (ARCTOS)
Mar 2025 – May 2025
6-DoF arm · Imitation learning on top of IK
  • 3D-printed and assembled a 6-DoF ARCTOS arm and modeled it in MuJoCo (MJCF).
  • Implemented an IK controller and scripted expert to generate cube-grasp trajectories as demos.
  • Built an imitation-learning pipeline (BC + DAgger) that outputs end-effector deltas and transferred it to the real arm with safety constraints.

DQN 2015 Reimplementation
Mar 2025
Atari environments · Human-level performance on Breakout & Carnival
  • Implemented DQN with a convolutional Q-network for high-dimensional visual inputs.
  • Added preprocessing (frame stacking, flicker removal, grayscale) to stabilize training and improve approximation.
  • Built a replay buffer with GPU-aware memory and tuned training to reach human-level scores.
